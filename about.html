Taجdeed

Our Mission

Taجdeed: Renewal and Revitalisation of Arabic Calligraphy and Cultural Heritage

A cultural project bridging the gap between tradition and modernity, introducing young Arab diaspora individuals in London to the valuable tradition of Arabic calligraphy through workshops and digital experiences.

Key Features

  • In-person Workshops: A series of 3 main workshops
    • Workshop 1: History of calligraphy in the Arab and Islamic world
    • Workshop 2: Traditional Arabic writing with cultural institutions
    • Workshop 3: Calligraphy+Graffiti - "Calligraphiti" workshops
  • Digital Expansion: Educational masterclasses and online seminars
  • Community Building: Platform connecting individuals with similar cultural backgrounds
  • Calligraphy Kits: Personalised kits including traditional calligraphy tools

Benefits

  • Preservation of intangible cultural heritage
  • Convenient online and in-person formats
  • Networking with like-minded individuals
  • Development of creative skills combining traditional and contemporary methods

Social Impact

  • Preserve Arabic art's relevance for new generations
  • Strengthen cultural identity for diaspora communities
  • Create community through shared cultural experiences
  • Encourage creativity by adapting traditional heritage to digital age
